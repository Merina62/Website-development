What we do at Yarsa Labs

We help build beautiful experiences that makes a part of the lives of our customers everyday.

Mobile Apps [Engineering]
Our apps have been downloaded by tens of millions of users around the world. People love our apps, and it feels good knowing we are making someone's life a bit easier every minute.

Mobile Games [Engineering]
Millions of people play our games every day. Games are complex; what's more complicated is creating games that don't give the device a fever. Making the games playable by everyone, and in uncertain conditions is the craft of our mobile game developers.

Web Services [Engineering]
Web and beyond, we build online services, APIs and pages that power our mobile apps, games, and experiences. Here at Yarsa Labs, we make all kinds of online experiences and power-houses to keep our lights green. Making the online service fast, reliable, accessible, and meeting the standards is the craft of our web developers.

Project Management [Management]
Managing a diverse team working together on a dozen different project at a time is not everyone's cup of coffee. Our product managers ensure everybody is engaged, and our team managers ensure nobody is stressed. Minimizing the technical debts, and meeting the objectives without burning out the team is their craft.

UI/UX Design [Design]
Our users come from different places and have different expectations. Some people have special needs, and some are really new to technology. It's the job of our User Experience Designers to ensure compatibility, accessibility, and simplicity in all our products. It's their craft to create simple, yet feature-rich products that our customers love.

Art and Graphics Design
Love at first sight is not a myth. Even before our users touch their screen, they hear and see what's on the screen. Our designers and sound engineers make sure our users fall in love with our products when they see them for the first time.
